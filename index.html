Field notes

Tri-Community Adult Learning Association is a nonprofit based in Stony Plain, Alberta, dedicated to providing adult learning opportunities. We offer programs and support to help adults build skills and connect with their community.

Rooted in Stony Plain since our founding. Programs designed by and for local adults. Free or low-cost services for all. Volunteer-driven with community support.
